Project 4

(Artist Sketch) In this project, I combined game software with a productivity software for a timer based on the Pomodoro technique. Study or work on your tasks for 25 minutes, then take a 5 minute break to play the alien-themed game. Navigate through the scenes using the GUI buttons or by using the key bindings:
'1' - intro
'2' - 25 minute countdown, study time
'3' or 'g' or 'G' - 5 minute gameplay
'4' or 'h' or 'H' - about/help information
'5' or 'e' or 'E' - end scene
